Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how postiz.rankia.cloud ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our service to connect and publish content to social media platforms, including TikTok.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect and process information necessary to provide the services of scheduling and publishing content. This includes:

  • Account Information: When you connect your TikTok account, we receive public profile details such as your username, display name, and avatar URL.
  • OAuth Tokens: We securely store authorization tokens provided by TikTok to publish content on your behalf. We do not have access to your TikTok password.
  • Media Content: We store the videos, images, and texts that you upload to our platform for scheduling and publishing.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Authenticate your identity and connect your social media profiles.
  • Publish scheduled video and photo posts to your TikTok account as requested by you.
  • Provide analytics and status reports for your published posts.

3. Data Sharing and Retention

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data or connected account tokens with any third parties. Your data is strictly kept within your private instance of postiz.rankia.cloud. You can disconnect your TikTok account at any time, which will immediately revoke our access and delete the associated tokens.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at rankia.cloud.
